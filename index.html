About

I’m an Electrical Engineering student (Class of 2026) who loves blending technology, art, and design. I build interactive systems that balance precision and play—embedded control and reliability on one side; storytelling, visuals, and user experience on the other.

Recent work includes a VR Space Station Museum, a bionic hand prototype, a face-muscle click interface, and a set of 3D modeling pieces. I care about clear documentation and accessibility so others can learn from and remix what I make.
